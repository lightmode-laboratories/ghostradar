## Features

- **Leaderboard** — Rank your contacts by messages, activity, balance, ghost index, and more
- **Multi-Source** — Analyze iMessage, WhatsApp, Snapchat, Instagram, Telegram, TikTok, and Messenger
- **Detailed Analytics** — Deep-dive into any contact with charts, heatmaps, and conversation metrics
- **Ghost Index** — See who's ghosting who with a unique scoring algorithm
- **Balance Score** — Measure the equilibrium of your conversations
- **Message Browser** — Browse full conversation history
- **Custom Periods** — Analyze any time range from 7 days to all time
- **Source Merging** — Combine contacts across messaging platforms
- **Privacy First** — 100% local processing, your data never leaves your device
