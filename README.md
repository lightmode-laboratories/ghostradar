## Connect all your messaging apps

Analyze conversations from **7 platforms** in one place:

**iMessage** · **WhatsApp** · **Snapchat** · **Instagram** · **TikTok** · **Telegram** · **Messenger**

GhostRadar uses local app data and chat exports. Everything is processed on your device. No server, no upload, no cloud.

<br>

## Privacy First

Your messages and contacts **never leave your Mac**. GhostRadar reads local app data and chat exports, processes everything on-device, and stores nothing remotely. No server, no upload, no cloud.

<br>

## A word of caution

GhostRadar gives you a superpower: seeing what no one else can see in your conversations. These insights can change how you see your relationships, and rediscovering forgotten conversations can hit harder than you'd expect. Use responsibly.
